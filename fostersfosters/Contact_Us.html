FAQ

Should I be worried when adopting?

While there is a big life change when adopting a new animal, don't let this dampen your excitement

How common are medical conditions?

We have found in our personal experience that most dogs have some sort of food allergy with the potential for more serous issues.

Are French Bulldogs good with children or other dogs?

Much like people, every dog is different. Frenchies are very affectionate and generally do well with other dogs. They can be prone to jumping so you will need to keep an eye on them around small children

Would you do it again?

Yes! Every day!
